1. I go to work

Japanese (Romaji): watashi wa shigoto ni ikimasu
Japanese: 私 は 仕事 に 行きます
Nepali: म काम मा जान्छु
English: I work to go

2. Please look at this

Japanese (Romaji): kore wo mite kudasai
Japanese: これ を 見て ください
Nepali: यो हेर्नु होस्
English: this look please

3. I do a lot of work at home

Japanese (Romaji): ie ni ooi shigoto wo shimasu
Japanese: 家 に 多い 仕事 を します
Nepali: घर मा धेरै काम गर्छु
English: home at many work do

4. When will you look at that?

Japanese (Romaji): itsu sore wo mimasu ka
Japanese: いつ それ を 見ます か
Nepali: कहिले त्यो हेर्नु हुन्छ
English: when that look will

5. I will do this

Japanese (Romaji): watashi wa kore wo shimasu
Japanese: 私 は これ を します
Nepali: म यो गर्नेछु
English: I this will-do
